Pink Cake

Description

Pink Cake is a festive, sweet dessert in Stardew Valley, often associated with birthdays and celebrations. It’s a colorful, fruity cake that restores a large amount of energy and health. In-game, it is noted for its charming decoration and festive appearance—“There's little heart candies on top.”

Ingredients

  • Melon
  • Wheat Flour
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients: Melon, Wheat Flour, Sugar, and Egg.
  2. Go to the kitchen in your upgraded farmhouse or use a Cookout Kit.
  3. Open the cooking menu and select Pink Cake from the list of available dishes.
  4. Press Cook to combine the ingredients and make the Pink Cake.
