Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic comfort food in Stardew Valley, beloved by many as a simple but satisfying meal. It restores a moderate amount of energy and a good amount of health. In-game, it is noted for being “an old favorite,” offering a hearty combination of pasta and fresh tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients: Wheat Flour and Tomato.
  2. Go to the kitchen in your upgraded farmhouse or use a Cookout Kit.
  3. Open the cooking menu and select Spaghetti from the list of available dishes.
  4. Press Cook to combine the ingredients and make the Spaghetti.
