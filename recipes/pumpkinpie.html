Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin Pie is a Stardew Valley dessert made by baking silky pumpkin cream inside a flaky, golden crust. It's a rich and hearty treat that restores a large amount of energy and health. In-game, it is noted for its smooth, creamy texture—“Silky pumpkin cream in a flaky crust.”

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin
  • Wheat Flour
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients: Pumpkin, Wheat Flour, Milk and Sugar.
  2. Go to the kitchen in your upgraded farmhouse or use a Cookout Kit.
  3. Open the cooking menu and select Pumpkin Pie from the list of available dishes.
  4. Press Cook to combine the ingredients and make the Pumpkin Pie.
