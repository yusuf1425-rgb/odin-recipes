Fish Taco

Description

Fish Taco is a Stardew Valley dish made by wrapping freshly cooked fish in a warm tortilla with a bit of spice. It's a simple but flavorful meal that restores a good amount of energy and health. In-game, it's known for its strong aroma—“It smells delicious.”

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Tortilla
  • Red Cabbage
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Collect all ingredients: Tuna, Tortilla, Red Cabbage and Mayonnaise
  2. Go to either the kitchen in your upgraded farmhouse or use a Cookout Kit.
  3. Open the cooking menu and select Fish Taco from the list of available dishes.
  4. Press Cook to combine the ingredients and make the Fish Taco.
